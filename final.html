This is my Final Project for CSC 106

Here is my final project. My partner and I decided to create a Jeopardy game with Disney themed questions.
Instructions

  • Click the start button to enter the game.
  • Once in the game, you will see categories along the top and questions ranging from point values of 100 - 400.
  • Click on a tile to open the question. You can only open each tile once.
  • When you click on a tile, a new screen will open up that states the question dn four options.
  • Click the right option and it will award you with points and take you to a correct screen.
  • Click the wrong option and you will not recieve points and it wil take you to a wrong screen.
  • From the correct or wrong screen, click the continue button to return to game screen.
  • Once all tiles have been opened, the program will take you to an end screen that states you final score.
  • I hope you enjoy this program and hope it challenges you knowledge on Disney.
